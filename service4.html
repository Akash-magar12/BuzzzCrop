Hybrid Poly Net House

Hybrid Poly Net House

Hybrid Poly Net House is a hybrid structure combination of polyhouse and shade net house which has poly film on the roof and shade net on the sides. It is mostly used for Nursery plantation and medicinal herbs farming. Poly Net houses protect the crop from cold and rain like polyhouse as well as allow air and sunlight to pass through from the sides of it like shade net houses so these type of polyhouse structure is best suited for dry and composite climatic zones where more difficult to maintain with an uncertain environment. Vatshal Green Environment provides installation and setup services for protected farming structures like hybrid customize polyhouse as the requirement of hi-tech agriculture projects.
