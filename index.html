Our Services

Flat Shade Net House

Flat Shade Net House consist of steel pipes hot-dip galvanized & enclosed with agro-shade nets, that let the required sunlight, humidity & air through the gaps. This setup makes it quite efficient in protecting crops.

Dome Shade Net House

Dome Shaped Net Houses provide the same benefits as Flat shade net houses, but they include a unique design that is tailored in size and dimensions to create an ergonomic & aesthetically appealing environment.

Wire Rope Net House

To ensure you get chemical-free food, we are using manure and vermicompost as fertilizers for nutrient supply and organic pesticides to control pests in your kitchen and fruit garden.

Hybrid Poly Net House

Hybrid Poly Net House offers an amalgamation of polyhouse and shade net house, with its roof covered in a poly film while the sides are draped in shade net. This is the perfect blend of both structures.

Naturally Ventillated Polyhouse

Greenhouses are perfect solutions to control the climatic conditions inside. They are typically made up of walls and roof with transparent materials such as glass or plastic, thus allowing adequate exposure to sunlight.

Climate Controlled Polyhouse

Our specialized Fan & Pad Polyhouse is a great way to get a headstart in setting up plantations, tissue plant labs, and medicinal crop-plantations; no matter if you're in an arid or non-arid region.
