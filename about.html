About Us

BuzzzCrop Agritech is a team with experience in all corners of farming working together to provide the best service to customers. We offering Hi-Tech solutions to traditional farming with suitable farming methods with new generation agriculture technology. We provide agriculture solution in protected cultivation, hydroponics farming, smart irrigation solutions, and agronomy services along with concept designing, consulting, protected structure developing, and customized agriculture project solution to farmers by our expertise in this field.

  • We're all about modern farming that's good for the planet and our plates. Our story started when we realized that farming today needs a fresh approach.
  • Our mission is simple: make farming smarter and kinder to the Earth. We want farms to grow more food without hurting our planet. We believe that modern farming, with its fancy tools and clever ideas, can do just that.
  • We're not just a bunch of words on a screen. Think of us as your farming buddies, here to share cool stuff.

Why Choose Us

We offer Home and Business gardening solutions.

Whether you are looking for a kitchen garden, Home garden, Landscape Design, Decorative plants, Trees, Shrubs, or garden accessories, we’ve got you covered. Established in 2010, we supply top quality garden plants and accessories. We can also give you expert advice and guidance on how to care for your plants throughout the year. Whether it is a small courtyard or large space, we can design, landscape or maintain your garden to the highest standard. We use only the best materials on the market. We aim to create a garden that you can enjoy whatever your budget or specification to acheive a unique outdoor space.

We Develop Fruitful Garden.

We have a experience in organic, chemical, free vegetable growing for your kitchen’s daily needs and fruits. We also develop protected structure for mini vegetables farm which can protect from higher sunlight, pests, winds and animals and generate eco-system for plants so you can get residue-free vegetable throughout the year. Our team regularly visit and manage structure, plants, irrigation system, pest control, fertigation, plant replacement, etc.

Comprehensive Services

From soil analysis and crop management to irrigation systems and pest control, our comprehensive range of services covers every aspect of modern agriculture, saving you time, effort, and resources.We understand that every farm is unique.
